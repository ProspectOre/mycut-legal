MyCut Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 10, 2026

MyCut ("we," "our," or "the app") is a commission tracking app for automotive sales professionals. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Summary

  • Your deal and commission data stays on your device and your private iCloud account
  • We collect anonymous analytics to improve the app
  • We show ads supported by Google AdMob
  • We do not sell your personal information

1. Data You Enter

Deal Information

You enter deal information including vehicle details, sale prices, costs, and customer names. This data is:

  • Stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
  • Optionally synced to your private iCloud account (if enabled)
  • Never sent to our servers
  • Never shared with third parties

Pay Plan Settings

Your commission rates, pack amounts, salary settings, and bonus tiers are stored the same way—locally and in your private iCloud if sync is enabled.

2. iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync:

  • Your data syncs across your Apple devices signed into the same iCloud account
  • Data is stored in Apple's CloudKit infrastructure
  • We cannot access your iCloud data
  • Apple's privacy policy governs iCloud storage: apple.com/legal/privacy

3. Analytics (TelemetryDeck)

We use TelemetryDeck to collect anonymous usage analytics. This helps us understand how the app is used and what features to improve.

What we collect:

  • App launches and feature usage (e.g., "deal created", "settings opened")
  • Device type and iOS version
  • Anonymous session identifiers

What we do NOT collect:

  • Your name, email, or contact information
  • Deal amounts, vehicle details, or customer names
  • Location data
  • Advertising identifiers

TelemetryDeck is privacy-focused and GDPR compliant. Learn more: telemetrydeck.com/privacy

4. Crash Reporting (Firebase Crashlytics)

We use Firebase Crashlytics to detect and fix app crashes. When a crash occurs, we receive:

  • Device model and iOS version
  • Crash stack traces (technical debugging information)
  • App state at time of crash

Crashlytics does not collect your personal data or deal information. Learn more: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5. Advertising (Google AdMob)

MyCut displays ads provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA) if you have allowed tracking
  • Device information
  • Approximate location (based on IP address)
  • Ad interaction data

You can limit ad tracking in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

6. Data Retention

  • Your data: Stored until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Analytics: Retained by TelemetryDeck for 12 months
  • Crash reports: Retained by Firebase for 90 days

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Export your data: Use the CSV export feature in Settings > iCloud & Data
  • Delete your data: Delete deals individually or use Developer Tools to clear all data
  • Disable iCloud sync: Toggle off in Settings > iCloud & Data
  • Limit ad tracking: iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Request data deletion: Contact us at the email below

8. Children's Privacy

MyCut is designed for automotive sales professionals and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We'll post significant changes in the app and update the effective date above.
