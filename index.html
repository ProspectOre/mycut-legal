MyCut Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 28, 2026

MyCut ("we," "our," or "the app") is a free commission tracking app for automotive sales professionals, supported by minimal advertising. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Summary

  • 100% Free: No subscription, no in-app purchases, all features unlocked
  • Your deal and commission data stays on your device and your private iCloud account
  • We collect anonymous analytics via Firebase Analytics to improve the app
  • We use Firebase Crashlytics to detect and fix crashes
  • We show minimal ads supported by Google AdMob to fund development
  • We do not sell your personal information

1. Data You Enter

Deal Information

You enter deal information including vehicle details, sale prices, costs, and customer names. This data is:

  • Stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
  • Optionally synced to your private iCloud account (if enabled)
  • Never sent to our servers
  • Never shared with third parties

Pay Plan Settings

Your commission rates, pack amounts, salary settings, and bonus tiers are stored the same way—locally and in your private iCloud if sync is enabled.

Demo Mode

If you use "Explore Demo" to preview the app:

  • Sample data is generated for demonstration purposes only
  • Demo data is automatically deleted when the app closes or restarts
  • Nothing from demo mode is stored permanently or synced
  • No ads are shown during demo mode

2. iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync:

  • Your data syncs across your Apple devices signed into the same iCloud account
  • Data is stored in Apple's CloudKit infrastructure
  • We cannot access your iCloud data
  • Apple's privacy policy governs iCloud storage: apple.com/legal/privacy

3. Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics)

We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand app usage and fix crashes.

Firebase Analytics collects:

  • App opens and feature usage events
  • Device model and iOS version
  • General usage patterns (screens viewed, buttons tapped)
  • Anonymous user engagement metrics

Firebase Crashlytics collects:

  • Crash stack traces (technical debugging information)
  • Device state at time of crash

Firebase does NOT collect:

  • Your deal amounts, vehicle details, or customer names
  • Personal identifying information

Learn more: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

MyCut displays ads provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA) if you have allowed tracking
  • Device information
  • Approximate location (based on IP address)
  • Ad interaction data

You can limit ad tracking in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Retention

  • Your data: Stored until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • Analytics: Retained by Firebase for 14 months
  • Crash reports: Retained by Firebase for 90 days

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Export your data: Use the CSV export feature in Settings > Data Management
  • Delete your data: Settings > Data Management > Delete All Deals or Delete All Data & Reset App
  • Disable iCloud sync: Toggle off in Settings > Data Management
  • Limit ad tracking: iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  • Request data deletion: Contact us at the email below

For Users in the European Economic Area (EEA)

Under GDPR, you also have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Data portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format (CSV export)
  • Object: Object to processing of your data for certain purposes
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

7. International Data Transfers

Your deal and pay plan data remains on your device and in your private iCloud account. However, our third-party service providers (Google AdMob, Firebase) may process anonymous analytics and advertising data in the United States or other countries. These providers maintain appropriate safeguards for international data transfers.

8. Children's Privacy

MyCut is designed for automotive sales professionals and is not intended for children under 13 (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We'll post significant changes in the app and update the effective date above.
